A Parkour Community in Scranton, Pennsylvania

Here at Scranton Parkour, we offer quality Parkour classes for both children and adults of all skill levels.

Why practice Parkour?

Even the very basics of parkour will change the way you see the world around you. A new dimension will become available to you. It's playfully called "Parkour Vision".

Very few worldwide disciplines have living founders. Parkour is one of them. And training with Parkour Endure will mean that you'll be kept up-to-date with all opportunities to train with these living legends. The founders of parkour travel the world coaching workshops, and you can train right alongside them.

Parkour Classes in Scranton, Pennsylvania

Here at Scranton Parkour, we offer quality Parkour classes for both children and adults of all skill levels. Our goal is to build a community that you can be excited to be a part of!

Why practice Parkour?

What parkour offers that most disciplines do not is an insight into the very basics of movement. Whether you are a star athlete, cross-training from a separate discipline, or just beginning your fitness training, there is something in parkour for you.

Strong Body - Strong Mind - Strong Spirit

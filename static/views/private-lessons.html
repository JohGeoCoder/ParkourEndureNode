Private Lessons in Scranton, Pennsylvania

Private lessons are popular among:

  • Parents with children who are not yet old enough for the Adult Classes. Give your children a head start for the kids classes that are in the works!
  • People cross-training from other disciplines and practices such as martial arts or sports.
  • People who want specialized instruction for specific goals.
  • Groups of friends who want to give parkour a shot together.

If you have a choice between training solo and training with a group, definitely train with a group whether it's with the group classes or you drag friends to a group private lesson. The opportunities to laugh and smile with each other during group sessions is what makes training worth it!

To set up group private lessons or solo private lessons, contact the head coach, John George. You can find his contact information on the Contact Us page.

Classes and lessons are conducted through Parkour Endure, LLC. And Parkour Endure considers anyone from ages 6 to 13 years old to be eligible for kids classes. If you or your child are 14 years of age or older, please look at our Adult Classes.

Kids Group Parkour Classes in Scranton

It is a child's natural instinct to explore and discover. Parkour can help your child learn to do just that in a structured and goal-oriented way that is both fun and engaging. Both confidence and creativity will skyrocket!

Explore Program (Ages 7-11)

Time:

Every Sunday at 3:00pm until 4:00pm.

Description:

Your child will learn to explore their surroundings in the form of games and group exercises. They will learn how to stand up to peer pressure and how to find fun in challenges.

Discover Program (Ages 12-14)

Time:

Every Sunday at 4:30pm until 6:00pm.

Description:

Entering their adolescent years, your child will be encouraged to seek and discover what their movement means to them. They will be introduced to the art of goal-setting and shown the value of patience and hard work.

Kids Enrollment

Classes with Parkour Endure are led by coaches certified through A.D.A.P.T. You are guaranteed the highest quality parkour coaching offered in the region.

To enroll your child, he or she must complete an Introductory Class

An Introductory Class is a one-on-one session with the coach and the prospective student. The parents are encouraged to be present so everyone can be briefed about what can be expected during the regular classes.

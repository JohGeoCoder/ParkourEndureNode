Parkour Classes in Scranton, Pennsylvania

Beginner Adult Classes (ages 14+, 2 hours)

2016 weekly classes begin on Sunday, April 3rd!

Come learn your true potential. Whether you take our class for fitness or for fun, you'll walk away with the ability to view your surroundings in a different light (or in the cases of night training, a lack thereof).

April Meeting Location:

Nay Aug Park. The closest address is 600 Arthur Avenue in Scranton, PA. Meet us near the Water Slide.

Time:

Every Sunday promptly at 3:00pm until 5:00pm. Be on time! We may leave the meeting area and it might be difficult to find us.

Bring:

  • Water
  • Exercise clothing like sweatpants or gym shorts
  • A small bag to carry your personal belongings
  • A signed Adult or Child waiver for your first class. For students 17 years of age or younger, a parent or legal guardian must be present when the waiver is turned in.
  • Your positive attitude :)

Price:

Classes are $20 for an individual. $15 for University and College students (must have student ID). Every time you bring someone new, you pay only $10 for that class. No monthly contract or obligations.

Classes with Parkour Endure are led by coaches certified through A.D.A.P.T. You are guaranteed the highest quality parkour coaching offered in the region. If it would be your first class with Parkour Endure, please call John at (717) 343-4727 so you know what you need to bring.

Free Play Sessions:

Students of the Beginner Adult classes and Private Lessons are invited to the weekly Free Play sessions.

These free sessions are pure camaraderie and exploration. Open training sessions, or "jams", are great opportunities to go back to the roots of how parkour started: outside, unorganized, being challenged by friends, and being challenged with friends.

Classes and lessons are conducted through Parkour Endure, LLC. And Parkour Endure considers anyone from ages 14 years and above to be eligible for adult classes. If you or your child are 13 years of age or younger, please look at our Kids Classes.
